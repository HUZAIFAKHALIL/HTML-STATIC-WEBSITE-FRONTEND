Product Details

Exterior

Toyota Corolla 2017 Exterior features a brand new more modern and premium design language. The front end houses sleek narrow side swept headlights, a large grille with a chrome accent and a trapezium air intake. The rear end houses sleek sharp side swept taillights, a large rear bumper and a chrome trim piece running the length of the trunk. All variants of the Toyota Corolla 2017 feature almost identical styling. Toyota Corolla exterior have unique design language that allows it to stand out amongst other cars in its class.

Interior

Toyota Corolla 2017 interior comprises of black and beige plastic trim pieces. The front and rear seats are covered in ivory fabric. Standard features of the base variant includes air conditioning, infotainment system, power steering and tachometer. while higher variants have satellite navigations, leather upholstery,multifunction steering wheel switches, two airbags, cruise control, front and rear camera, paddle shift and sunroof. Toyota Corolla 2017 cabin is very spacious, practical and luxurious

Engine

(Xli,GLi) 1.3 Liter DOHC 16 Valve VVT-I 85bhp@6000RPM (Altis) 1.6 Liter DOHC 16 Valve Dual VVT-I 120bhp@6000RPM (Altis Grande, Altis) 1.8 Liter DOHC 16 Valve Dual VVT-I 140bhp@6000RPM

Gearbox

5 speed Manual Transmission (XLi, GLi) 4 Speed Super ECT Automatic Transmission (GLi, Altis) 6 Speed M/T (Altis Grande) 7 speed Super CVTi A/T (Altis Grande)

Mileage

Toyota Corolla 2017 can achieve an average mileage of between 12KM/L to 14KM/L making for an estimated driving range of between 660KM to 770KM from the sedans 55-liter fuel tank.

Competitor

The main Toyota Corolla 2017 competitors are the Honda City Aspire, Honda Civic and Suzuki Liana.
