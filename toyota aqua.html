Product Details

Exterior

Toyota Aqua exterior features a unique sporty design language. The front end houses sleek narrow upswept LED headlights, a large hexagon grille and standard angular trapezium styled fog lights. The rear end houses long vertical side swept taillights with circular inserts, a large rear bumper, and a standard hatchback. The facelift variant of the Toyota Aqua featured a series of cosmetic upgrades. The front end houses new sharper angular upswept headlights, a larger more aggressively designed front bumper with a large hexagon grille and dual standard circular fog lights. The interior features metallic HVAC vents and a redesigned climate control cluster while the rear end houses redesigned taillights. All variants of the Toyota Aqua feature identical styling in every aspect. The overall exterior of the 1st Generation Toyota Aqua features a fairly unique and distinctive design language that can stand out among other cars in its class.

Interior

The interior of Toyota Aqua compromises of black plastic trim pieces. The front and rear seats are covered in fabric. Standard features on the One include automatic climate control, 4-speaker stereo speaker system, 3.5-inch multi-information display, 4-way power driver seat and 4-way power passenger seat, multifunction steering wheel, keyless entry, 9 airbags, and a 12V accessory port. Standard features on the Two include satellite navigation with a 6.1-inch multi-information display as standard, 6-speaker stereo audio system as standard and split folding rear seats. The Three variant features alloy wheels as standard, larger tires, side impact airbags and power drive seats as standard. Options include satellite navigation with a 6.1-inch multi-information display, 6-speaker stereo speaker system, power moonroof, reversing camera, touch tracer display, cruise control, 6-way power driver seat and leather upholstery. The overall interior of the 1st Generation Toyota Aqua features a decent amount of interior space for a hatchback of its exterior dimensions. It also offers a decent level of comfort and interior refinement.

Engine

1.5 Liter DOHC 16 Valve Inline-4

Gearbox

CVT Automatic Transmission

Competitor

The main competitors of the 1st Generation Toyota Aqua are the Honda Insight, Toyota Prius and the Lexus CT200h.
