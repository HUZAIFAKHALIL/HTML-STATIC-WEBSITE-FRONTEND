Product Details

Exterior

Daihatsu Mira new model exterior features a very familiar design language to many other kei cars. The front end houses trapezium styled side swept headlights, a rectangular grille, and circular fog lights. The rear end houses long narrow vertical stacked taillights and a standard case hatchback. The 5-door hatchback features identical styling to the 3-door hatchback apart from being slightly longer to accommodate the rear doors. The overall exterior of the Daihatsu Mira features a fairly unique design language that can differentiate itself from its rivals.

Interior

The 2016 Daihatsu Mira interior compromises the use of light gray plastic trim pieces. The front and rear seats are covered in black or beige fabric. Standard features on the TL variant includes air conditioning, dual front airbags, power steering and a stereo speaker audio system. Features on the higher end TX variant includes keyless entry. Features on the higher end X and L variants include power windows and front fog lights. Options include satellite navigation, leather steering wheel, leather upholstery, Smart Drive Package, front fog lights and traction control. The overall interior of 2006 Daihatsu Mira offers a good amount of interior space for a kei car of its exterior dimensions. It also offers a good level of comfort and interior refinement.
