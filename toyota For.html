Exterior

The exterior dimensions of 2nd generation of Toyota Fortuner is aesthetically revamped. The head lights of the vehicle have LED lights in them which further adds to the beauty of the car. The rear lights are much like the latest LX570, stretched and aggressive. The latest Toyota Fortuner 2017 has hydraulic trunk and bonnet. These hydraulic features are a blessing in SUVâ€™s as they are very heavy and it gets difficult to hold them. Both the front and rear bumpers are large enough with bulbs installed in them. It is a common trend to see large bumpers these days as it not only reduces the weight of the vehicle but also helps in better acceleration.

Interior

The interior of the vehicle is better than the previous version with better curves and edges.The vehicle is passenger friendly and it has a cup holder for the rear passenger in the hand rest found between the seats. The vehicle is equipped with 7 inch LCD screen. For further comfort for passengers at the back, AC vents are available at the back too. Seats at the back can be folded to increase the space for luggage. The steering of the vehicle is somewhat similar to Toyota Hilux Revo. The other driving amenities are Paddle shifts, all terrain perfomance switch, multiple cooling station, cool box, steering switches, cruise control, smart driving mode, illuminated controls, smart connectors, rear air conditioning and LED room lamp. Toyota Fortuner 2017 interior cabin is very spacious and practical

Engine

In Pakistan Toyota Fortuner comes in one engine option 2.7 Litre DOHC 16 Valve Chain Drive with Dual VVTi Engine

Color

The 2nd Generation Toyota Fortuner is offered in 7 colors Super White, Attitude Black, Silver Metallic, Graphite, Bronze Mica, Strong Blue and Phantom Brown

Competitor

There is no direct competitor of the vehicle in Pakistan, as in this particular price range, there is no brand new SUV being offered. However, one can say that Mitsubishi Pajero is a competitor of fortuner, but the SUV is not available readily in the market.
