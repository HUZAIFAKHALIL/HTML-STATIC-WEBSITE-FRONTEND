Product Details

Exterior

The Suzuki Mehran 2017 exterior features a fairly box like design. Mehran Car front end features the use of trapezium shaped headlights with a grille that features multiple rectangular shaped openings on one side and a sealed end on the other along with standard black bumpers. The rear end of car houses slightly angular squared shaped taillights, a standard hatchback along with a standard black bumper. On the higher end Mehran VXR variants, the Mehran Car comes equipped with color matched bumpers and full wheel covers. The overall exterior of the Suzuki Mehran 2017 features a restrained and minimalistic design language that is typical for a car designed in the eighties.

Interior

Suzuki Mehran 2017 interior comprises the use of gray tone plastic trim pieces. The interior is very minimalistic as many aspects of the car are left exposed and everything including windows and the seat adjustment controls is operated manually. The Mehran Car lacks basic features such as central locking, steering adjustment and rear seat belts. The front and rear seats are both wrapped in vinyl. Standard features on the Car Mehran include a heater while the higher end VXR variant features air conditioning and fabric seats. The overall interior of the Suzuki Mehran 2017 features a lack of interior space in both the front and rear for a hatchback of its exterior dimensions, it also offers a lack of comfort and interior refinement.

Engine

0.8 Liter OHC 6 Valve Inline-3

Gearbox

4 Speed Manual Transmission

Mileage

The Suzuki Mehran 2017 gives an average mileage of 10 KM/L in city making for an estimated range of 300 KM from its 30-litre fuel tank and 13KM/L on the highway making for an estimated range of 390 KM on a highway.

Color

The Suzuki Mehran 2017 is offered in a choice of 4 colors Graphite Grey, Solid White, Pearl Red and Silky Silver.

Competitor

The main competitors of the Suzuki Mehran 2017 are the Hyundai Santro, Suzuki Alto, Daihatsu Charade, Nissan Otti, and Daihatsu Mira.
