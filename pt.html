HONDA BR-V 2017

PKR 23.3 lacs

GET THE CAR

Product Details

Exterior

The Honda BR-V features active solid motion exterior design. The headlights and front grills are designed to highlight the sporty look. The front facia is comprised of sporty grills nestled between the sleek headlights having project lamps. The rear end also features the use of chrome with a bar running across the centre giving eloquent look to the posterior part Roof rails adds more extravaganza to Honda BRV. The general shape of BR-V is that of a typical subcompact SUV and due to sleek exterior design and unique styling BR-V feels very opulent on the road.

Interior

The interior of the Honda BR-V features the classic 7 seater layer arranged among three rows. The interior comes as standard in Sand Beige Leather. Standard features on the base variant include driver side airbag, power windows, infotainment system, driver side height adjustable lever, second row reclining deats, navigation system, rear air vents, digital climate control, rear view camera and retractable side mirrors with indicator lamps. The overall cabin of BR-V is very spacious, practical and luxurious.

Engine

The Honda BRV was launched with 1.5 Litre SOHC 16 Valve iVTEC Engine

Color

The First Generation Honda BRV was offered in a choice of 5 colors Crystal Black Pearl, Lunar Silver Metallic, Sporty Blue, Taffeta White and Urban Titanium.

Competitor

Suzuki Vitara, Honda Vezel and Honda HR-V

Pros & Cons

Pros:
Comfortable, Luxurious with high tech driving aids, Extremely spacious and Practical.
Cons:
Only one airbag; Traction control and Cruise control not available.
