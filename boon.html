Product Details

Exterior

The exterior of the 2010 Daihatsu Boon features a fairly unique and distinctive design language. The front end houses sleek sharp upswept headlights, a trapezium shaped black colored grille, a rectangular style air intake and dual standard fog lights. The sides feature flared wheel arches. The rear end houses stacked vertical side swept taillights, the rear windscreen wiper and a standard case hatchback. All variants of the Daihatsu Boon feature identical styling in every aspect. The overall exterior of the 1st Generation Daihatsu Boon features a fairly unique and distinctive design language that is able to stand out amongst its competition.

Interior

The interior of the 1st Generation Daihatsu Boon compromises of light grey and beige plastic trim pieces. The front and rear seats are covered in fabric. Standard features on the CL Variant include air conditioning, power windows, power steering, power lock doors, stereo speaker audio system and a tachometer. Features on the higher end CX variant include front Xenon headlights, and side impact airbags. Options include satellite navigation, a panoramic sunroof, automatic climate control and leather upholstery. The overall interior of the 2010 Daihatsu Boon features a good amount of interior space for a hatchback of its exterior dimensions.

Mileage

The Daihatsu Boon 1st Generation is able to achieve an average of up to 20KM/L making for an estimated driving range of up to 800KM from the subcompact hatchbacks 40-liter fuel tank.

Competitor

The main competitors of the Daihatsu Boon 2010 are the Toyota Yaris, Suzuki Wagon R, Suzuki Cultus, FAW V2 and the Honda Fit.
