Product Details

Exterior

The exterior of the 1st Generation Daihatsu Cuore features a relevantly restrained designed language. The front end houses angular trapezium headlights, a small front bumper with multiple air intakes and a small polished silver colored oval grille. The rear end houses angular rectangular styled side swept taillights, a large outward sticking rear bumper and a standard case hatchback. All variants of the Daihatsu Cuore feature identical styling in every aspect. The overall exterior of the 1st Generation Daihatsu Cuore features a relatively minimalistic design language that is familiar for a Japanese hatchback designed in the early 2000's.

Interior

The interior of the 1st Generation Daihatsu Cuore features the use of dark grey plastic trim pieces. The front and rear seats are covered in fabric. Standard features on the CL variant include air conditioning and a stereo speaker system. Features on the higher end CX variant include cup holders, folding rear seats, power lock doors, power steering and power windows.
