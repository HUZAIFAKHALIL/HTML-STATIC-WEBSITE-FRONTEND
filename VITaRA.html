Product Details

Exterior

Suzuki Vitara comes with a stylish exterior that is second to none. It has painted alloy rims, chrome grille on front, roof spoiler, front and rear door visors, keyless entry, flexible front and rear mud flaps, silver side-body moulding, tail lamp garnish.

Interior

Vitara facelift in Pakistan comes with a huge 10.1” touchscreen infotainment system that captivates attention as soon as you enter the vehicle. There’s a pretty attractive climate control system. Once the desired temperature is set, the airflow and outlets are automatically selected and adjusted to maintain a uniform temperature throughout the cabin. There are many handy storage spaces located at various points across the vehicle. The panoramic sunroof of new Vitara consists of two glass panels, both of which can be slid. Moreover, for big families and for travelling, there’s plenty of luggage space, to be exact 375 liters. The meter cluster, tilt and telescopic steering wheel, paddle shifters, cruise control and automatic wipers and lights are some of the prominent features you can enjoy, as you drive the all new Suzuki Vitara.

Engine

1.6 Liter DOHC 16 Valve Engine

Gearbox

4 Speed Manual Transmission

Competitor

The main competitors of New Suzuki Vitara include Honda Vezel, Honda HR-V and Toyota C-HR, Audi Q2 and BMW X1.
