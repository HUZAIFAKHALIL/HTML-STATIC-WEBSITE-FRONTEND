Exterior

The 3rd Generation of cultus has a compact hatchback design. The overall exterior of suzuki cultus is unique and well proportioned and it can easily outrace other imported hatchbacks available in Pakistan

The interior of cultus is highly poised and sophisticated fullfilling all the needs of family hatchback The interior comes as a standard Black coloured fabric. The features of 3rd Generation of cultus are Dual front airbags,Power Windows,CD player with AUX and USB slot and Power mirrors The overall cabin of the cultus is very spacious and practical
